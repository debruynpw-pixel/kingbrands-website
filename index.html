The Group

An Established House of Everyday Quality

King Brands Group is a South African retail, wholesale and distribution house. We build brands people trust, manufacture to exacting standards, and move product from our Chamdor warehouse to shelves nationwide.

Every brand under our crown carries the same promise — quality products, every day — from the golden syrups of Gold Joy to the pure honey of Nectarly.
